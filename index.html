About Me

I'm a PhD candidate in Electrical and Computer Engineering at Western University. My research focuses on developing robust, interpretable machine learning systems for early fault detection in real-world applications. I specialize in graph neural networks, time-series analysis, and explainable AI.

Currently working on spatio-temporal GNN models for leak detection in CO₂-based refrigeration systems with counterfactual explainability at Neelands Group (Mitacs Phase II).
