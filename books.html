Book Notes

Antifragile (N. N. Taleb)

Thesis: Systems can benefit from volatility and stressors.

  • Prefer options with bounded downside and unbounded upside.
  • Remove fragilities before adding complexity.
  • Barbell strategies for research exploration vs exploitation.
Deep Work (Cal Newport)

Thesis: Focused, distraction‑free blocks produce outsized results.

  • Time‑block and protect focus windows.
  • Track lead indicators (hours of deep work) not just lagging outputs.
  • Ritualize startup and shutdown routines.
